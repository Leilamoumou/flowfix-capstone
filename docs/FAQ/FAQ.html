Pricing & Estimates

We have a flat rate for our plumbing services. Although, for specific plumbing services (depending on the complexity of the job), the price may vary. It is best to check our pricing page for the most up-to-date information.

Our pricing is based on several factors: the type of service, the parts needed, and the estimated labor time. For standard repairs, we often use a flat-rate pricing model so there are no surprises. For more unique or extensive projects, we provide a detailed quote following an on-site inspection. This ensures you only pay for the specific work your property/home requires.

To make the process as smooth as possible, FlowFix accepts a variety of payment options. You can pay via credit or debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay, or traditional methods such as check and cash. Payment is typically due upon completion of the service unless otherwise arranged for large-scale projects.
