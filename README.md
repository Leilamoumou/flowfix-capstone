# FlowFix

## Product Vision
Whether you're a plumber who is tired of larger corporations taking a big chunk of your pay or someone in need of a proper plumbing emergency fix, Flowfix is for you! Our AI-powered tool is designed to connect those on either end of the spectrum to their best match. It's best-suited for homeowners, renters, landlords, and other realtor/property managers.
